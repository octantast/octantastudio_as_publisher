Formative Event

After birth, Vitazia faced difficulties learning to speak, with physical speech issues, which led her to compensate by studying speech techniques, including pick-up techniques like "closer-further," "needles," and other unethical methods. As compensation, she also learned to communicate with the world through symbols and images, including using Senet, where each move acts as a phrase or expression instead of the usual.

Beliefs & Goals

She believes that her distracting beauty and, in some sense, her ambiguity and mystique are her allies, as they allow her to observe and gather information without stepping out of her safe zone. She thinks that manipulation can compensate for her inability to speak directly.

Turning Point

The need to stand up for the protagonist to protect him from the lodge. The necessity to choose a side, rather than maintain neutrality and pretend to be innocent.

Future Development

She will learn to express her needs directly and discover that it's often easier than coming up with complex plans and creating situations through others.
